This repository focuses on home mosquito treatment, offering practical solutions and curated backlinks to help homeowners eliminate breeding grounds and reduce mosquito populations.

Mosquitoes can spread dangerous diseases such as dengue, chikungunya, and malaria. Even small amounts of standing water can become breeding sites, making prevention a top priority.
